Cancer awareness for 50,000 kids
SpeakUp/HablaClaro - E.R.I.C. in Venezuela
Give

We are founders of the Ultimate in Venezuela and Latin-American and we have 35 years of experience of developing and promoting the Ultimate. We are convinced that our beloved sport is the tool for the education of young people with values our country needs. A change of mentality and through our Ultimate clinic we teach fundamental values for the development of a society. We believe in mutual respect, dialogue in the resolution of conflicts, teamwork is the key to success, as well as teaching motivation to achieve through learning. Throwing and catching can be a life lesson to build any goal in life, and all this we do under a healthy fun environment which kids love.  

Inspired by the E.R.I.C. Speak Up tour, and with approval from the organization, we are creating the Habla Claro tour. We see this as an opportunity to be an agent of change. Adding to all our experience the message that early recognition of cancer is critical for youth and that it can save a life, is great. We want to be part of the solution, bringing positive news in the middle of the social, economic and political chaos in our country.   The AC CAOBOS ULTIMATE CLUB DEPORTIVO is E.R.I.C.  Ambassador in Venezuela, we have the experience and knowledge to implement the E.R.I.C Speak Up Tour in the Venezuelan schools and are committed to reaching 50,000 kids in four years.  

 

We are the pioneers in teaching the Ultimate in our country, we have implemented clinics in schools and we have developed a simple method to start the practice of the sport,  

 

We designed and validated The Ultimate Trainers Manual which was sponsored by the WFDF Grants Program 2015, for our Project Venezuela Ultimate Program 2013-2017. Our method teaches the basics that a kids should know about Ultimate and Spirit of the game, as well as throwing and catching the disc for which we have benefited 500 students in 2016; we can spreading the educational message that early recognition of cancer symptoms is critical. Being active, being body aware and speaking up, is a great message and the sport of Ultimate and Spirit of the Game are great ways to deliver that message to today’s youth.

 

Venezuela is experiencing the worst political, economic and social crisis of its history, the GIRA HABLA CLARO (Speak Up Tour Venezuela) is an opportunity to be agents of change, generating   positive result in the promotion of the Ultimate in Venezuela and impact on the lives of 50,000 students in 2020.

 

Mission

Educating young students of the Venezuelan Schools by touring clinics Ultimate spreading the message about Spirit of the game is the perfect tools to teach about cancer prevention and strengthening social values such as quality of live.  

 

Vision

By 2020 The Ultimate Clinic GIRA HABLA CLARO will have spread the E.R.I.C Speak Up cancer message, as well as taught ultimate and spirit of the game to 50,000 young Venezuelans.
